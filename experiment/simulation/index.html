Bulking of Fine Aggregate

Objective:

To determine the bulking of a given sample of fine aggregate


Apparatus used:

Apparatus used: A 250 ml measuring cylinder , Fine aggregate, Tamping rod having a diameter of 6 mm , Steel ruler .



STEP  1 
 Collect the sand sample in a container.  

STEP  2 
  Take a measuring cylinder and fill its 2/3 part with the collected sand sample.  

STEP  3 
 Gently level off the top surface of sand using a steel ruler.  

STEP  4 
  Measure the height of loose sand and note it down.  

STEP  5 
  Transfer the sand from the measuring cylinder into a cleaned beaker.  

STEP  6 
 Refill half of the cylinder with water.  

STEP  7 
 Pour the sand from the beaker into the cylinder. Add it in 3 parts and Stir the poured sand in the cylinder gently with a tamping rod having a diameter of 6 mm.
